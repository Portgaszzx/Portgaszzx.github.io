1 Board game

“Crisis Public Relations” is based on the board game “Hostage Negotiation”, combined with the background of the real Chengdu high school student falling from the building, and applied the knowledge of communication to make production. This board game is set in the event of a crisis of public opinion, requiring players to play the role of the government and apply various methods to solve the crisis of public opinion.

2 Emergency-themed Serious Game Design

The purpose of this article is to study the development of serious games and try to design a serious game. The concept of serious games was proposed by the educational and sociologist Clark.C. Albert in 1970, and its core extension and application have been developed in the 21st century. This paper summarizes the development process of the concept of serious games at home and abroad, finds that there is a lack of serious games with medical themes as the background in China, and designs and improves a serious game in which the audience plays the role of 120 dispatchers through the waterfall development method, so as to popularize the relevant first aid knowledge.
