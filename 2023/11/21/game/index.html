Board game

1 Introduction

“Crisis Public Relations” is based on the board game “Hostage Negotiation”, combined with the background of the real Chengdu high school student falling from the building, and applied the knowledge of communication to make production. This board game is set in the event of a crisis of public opinion, requiring players to play the role of the government and apply various methods to solve the crisis of public opinion.

2 Details

2.1 The goal of the game

The player takes on the role of the government, with the aim of gaining more public support until the public opinion crisis is resolved.
The player wins the game when all of the following objectives are met:

  • There are no wandering masses in the wandering zone
  • At least half of the masses choose to support you
  • The public opinion incident was resolved

The player immediately loses the game when any of the following conditions are met:

  • More than half of the masses are on your side
  • The public opinion incident was not resolved
  • There isn’t a single crisis card for players to draw on during the event progression phase
