Cat express

Basic information

Game: Cat express

Engine: Unity

Date: Aug, 2022

Download: https://brandon-official.itch.io/cat-express

Cat Express is a puzzle game based on the Snake mechanic, where the player takes on the role of a cat that eventually reaches the end of the line with the help of eating fruits and other means.

video:

Gameplay:

Length control, resource management, and gravity are the three pillars of the game, and a number of mechanisms have been designed to address these three points, which are represented in the first, second, and third major ones, respectively.

  • Eat cat food to increase length

  • Split the body to solve the puzzle collaboratively

  • Dye the corresponding color to pass through a specific area
