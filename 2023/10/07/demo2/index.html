Galaxy Port

Basic information

Game: Galaxy Port

Engine: Unity

Date: Oct 2022

Download:https://brandon-official.itch.io/galaxyport

Button Guide

space : jump

Arrow down key：roll

A/D : menu switching

mouse left：Interact

video：

Game Concept

Players will take on the role of an “interstellar mailman” to travel between planets with the help of an ejection bin, overcoming various obstacles, unlocking new cool abilities, exploring unknown worlds in the starry sky, and gradually discovering the truth of the “apocalypse”.

Game Play

Manipulation

“Easy to understand, hard to master”, I want players to be able to quickly familiarize themselves with the game’s operation, so I only set up two core keys, jump and roll, and by using jump and roll, many obstacles can be designed against.
